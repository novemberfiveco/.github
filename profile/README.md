[November Five](https://novemberfive.co/) is a digital product studio based in Antwerp, Belgium. More than fifteen years of shipping digital solutions taught us one thing: engineering is mostly about culture. We operate in small teams with real ownership, not a "someone else will catch it" mentality. Engineers own their work end to end, from architecture decisions to what breaks at 6pm on a Friday.

At November Five, we set non-negotiables and hold them. We invest in the people who carry the standard, not just the ones who meet it. And we share what we think and build.
